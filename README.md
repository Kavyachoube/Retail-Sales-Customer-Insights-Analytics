# 🛒 Retail Sales & Customer Insights Analytics

> An end-to-end Data Analytics project that transforms raw retail sales data into actionable business insights using **SQL Server, Python, and Power BI**.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Retail businesses generate thousands of transactions every day. However, raw transactional data alone cannot support strategic business decisions.

This project analyzes a retail sales dataset to uncover insights related to:

- 📈 Sales Performance
- 💰 Profitability
- 🛍 Product Performance
- 👥 Customer Behavior
- 🌍 Regional Performance
- 🚚 Shipping Efficiency
- 🎯 Executive KPIs

The goal is to help business managers make data-driven decisions through SQL analysis, Python-based exploratory data analysis, and interactive Power BI dashboards.

# 📊 Key Insights

✔ Technology products generated the highest revenue.

✔ High discounts significantly reduced profitability.

✔ A small group of customers contributed a large share of sales.

✔ Several high-sales products generated low profit due to excessive discounts.

✔ Regional sales varied considerably, highlighting expansion opportunities.

✔ Standard Class was the most frequently used shipping mode.

✔ Certain products consistently generated financial losses.

---

# 💼 Business Recommendations

- Optimize discount strategies to improve profitability.
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing customer segments.
- Increase inventory for high-demand products.
- Improve logistics for slower shipping methods.
- Reassess pricing of consistently loss-making products.
- Expand business presence in underperforming regions.
- Strengthen customer retention programs for high-value customers.
